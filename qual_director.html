Governance

Qualification of Board of Directors

Directors are expected to have qualifications that reflect the following qualities:

Understand the job requirements of a director and can contribute to the Board of Director’s performance.

Comfortable and effective in senior level settings.

Can listen and present with an open mind.

Makes timely decisions.

Respects the Board of Director’s process.

Willing to serve on committees or task forces.

Have extensive professional experience with significant leadership accomplishments in business, government, academia, philanthropy, or the nonprofit sector.

Commitment to and understanding of IITRF purposes and objectives.

Have skills and natural affinity for cultivating relationships and persuading, convening, facilitating, and building consensus skills among diverse individuals.

Personal qualities of integrity, credibility, and a passion for making a difference.
